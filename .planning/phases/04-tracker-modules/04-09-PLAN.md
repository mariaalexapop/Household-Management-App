**1. src/app/(app)/costs/page.tsx** -- Server Component:

- Accept optional `?year=2026` search param (default: current year).
- Calculate yearStart as Jan 1 of selected year.
- Parallel cost aggregation queries (3 queries):
  - Car service costs: `SELECT to_char(service_date, 'YYYY-MM') as month, COALESCE(SUM(cost_cents), 0) as total FROM service_records WHERE household_id = ? AND service_date >= yearStart GROUP BY month`
  - Insurance premiums: `SELECT to_char(next_payment_date, 'YYYY-MM') as month, COALESCE(SUM(premium_cents), 0) as total FROM insurance_policies WHERE household_id = ? AND next_payment_date >= yearStart GROUP BY month`
  - Electronics purchases: `SELECT to_char(purchase_date, 'YYYY-MM') as month, COALESCE(SUM(cost_cents), 0) as total FROM electronics WHERE household_id = ? AND purchase_date >= yearStart GROUP BY month`
